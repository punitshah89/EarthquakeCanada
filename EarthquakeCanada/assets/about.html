Earthquake Canada

Description :- It helps to keep track about earthquakes that occurred within the last 7 days, 30 days or an year ago within Canada. It also facilitates the user to view History.

Features:-

  • Notifications for the latest earthquakes occurred.
  • Different filter to sort(ascending and descending) according to date, magnitude and province
  • Easy to Search based on Earthquake title, location, date/Time and province
